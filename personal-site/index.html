Camp Half-Blood is a fictional summer camp from the book series Percy Jackson and the Olympians by Rick Riordan. It is where the modern-day Greek Demigods (children of the Greek gods) go for training and just a safe place to live.

Welcome to Camp Half-Blood - Where Adventure and Learning Meet!

At Camp Half-Blood, we are passionate about creating memorable summer experiences for children and young adults. Our camp is more than just a place; it's a community where kids can grow, learn, and make lifelong friends while having a blast.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and fun-filled environment where children can discover their full potential. We believe that every child is unique and has their own talents and interests waiting to be explored. Our aim is to help them flourish by offering a wide range of activities that promote personal growth, teamwork, and a deep appreciation for the great outdoors.

Why Choose Us?

Experienced and Caring Staff: Our team consists of experienced counselors, educators, and professionals who are dedicated to creating a positive and inclusive camp environment.

Diverse Program Options: We offer a diverse array of activities, from traditional campfire songs and crafts to outdoor adventures like hiking, swimming, and nature exploration.

Safety First: The safety of our campers is our top priority. We have strict safety protocols, trained lifeguards, and certified staff members to ensure a secure camp experience.

Environmental Stewardship: We instill in campers a love for nature and respect for the environment. Our campsite is chosen to immerse campers in the beauty of the outdoors.

Lasting Friendships: Campers form lifelong friendships, learn to be more self-reliant, and develop crucial social skills.

Join Us Today!

Whether your child is a first-time camper or returning for another summer of adventure, Camp Half-Blood is the perfect place to create lasting memories and personal growth. We invite you to explore our programs, get to know our team, and discover why families choose us year after year.

Camp Half-Blood is a sanctuary for demigods and nature spirits. The campgrounds are surrounded by a magical barrier that keeps monsters and mortals out. The camp began back in Ancient Greece with the first campers brought by Apollo for Chiron to teach and train. The magical borders were put in place after a large monster attack nearly wiped the Camp out of existence back in Ancient Greece, though luckily with the help of some former campers that were able to come to their aid in time the current campers were able to fend off the attack.

Roughly around the year 2000 a group of demigods were arriving at Camp with monsters on their tail and unfortunately one of them, Thalia Grace, Daughter of Zeus, gave her life protecting her companions. Her father then preserved her life force in the form of a large pine tree on the border of Camp. Her tree reinforced the strength of the magical barriers. Six years later, a group of campers went on a quest to retrieve the Golden Fleece after Thalia's tree was poisoned. They returned victorious and the Fleece not only healed the tree, but brought Thalia back to life. Our dragon Peleus has guarded the Fleece and borders since.

As Camp Half-Blood grew it was moved from the base of Mount Pelion, Chiron's home, to the base of Mount Olympus as well as gaining a multitude of Satyr guides to lead young half-bloods safely to Camp. Since the first relocation, the Camp has moved with the gods as the core of Western Civilization traveled around the world. It is currently in America, with Camp Half-Blood located in Long Island, New York.

Meet Our Staff

Our esteemed Camp Director is Mr. D, or as you may know from the myths, Dionysus, the god of wine and theatre. He has been sent here for 50 years before we will be graced with a new director.

Chiron, yes, the Chiron from all the famous stories, is our Activities Director and is the one that all the campers look to for advice and direction. He has been granted his wish of staying around until he is no longer needed, and with how things are going, that may be for the foreseeable future.

Argus is our head of security. He was created by Hera back in Ancient Greece with a hundred eyes all over his body, helping him keep an eye on the safety of our campers.

Along with Argus, we employ three harpies to help with security so the campers can sleep safely in their cabins at night as well as clean up after each meal along with anyone on dish duty.

Peleus is also part of our security, protecting the borders of Camp as well as the Golden Fleece.
