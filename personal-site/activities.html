Housing

The Campers sleep in cabins with their godly half-siblings.

Each cabin houses children with the same godly parent and is customized to their skill sets, interests, and/or powers.

Session Dates

We do have the option for year round attendance and operate as a homeschool alternative.

Summer

The Summer session runs from the second week of June through the first weekend of August.

At the end of each summer the campers receive a bead with a representation of the most notable moment at Camp that year.

Fall

The Fall session runs from the second week of August through the first weekend of December.

Winter

The Winter session runs from the second weekend of December through first weekend of January. For year-round campers, through the last weekend of February.

Spring

The Spring session runs from the first week of March through the first week of June.
